What is Listrify?

Listrify helps Web3 projects secure centralized exchange (CEX) listings by handling compliance, exchange introductions, liquidity support and listing campaigns. We prepare projects end-to-end so exchanges accept submissions and listings perform well post-launch.

  • Exchange Introductions & Professional Submissions
  • Liquidity & Market-Making Support
  • PR, AMA sessions & Community Growth Campaigns
